My name and pronouns

Name.
My full name is Yana Khusanova. In casual communications you can address me by my first name Yana or Yanou (pronounced with an accent on last syllable). You can write my first name in any way you want as long as it reads correctly in your language. English,French: Yana/Yanou; German,Esperanto: Jana, Janú; Italian: Iana/Ianú; Spanish: Llana/Llanú.

Pronouns (they/she).
My preferred pronouns are they/them but feel free to use any pronouns referring to me.
